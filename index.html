About me

I work under the supervision of Stéphane Robin and Christophe Ambroise at the French Mathématiques et Informatique appliquées Lab (MIA-Paris). Using statistical modelling and dedicated algorithms, we infer species interaction networks in collaboration with ecology and biology experts.

I am currently looking for a Post-doc in statistical ecology. Please find my CV here (english version, updated 27/02/2020).
